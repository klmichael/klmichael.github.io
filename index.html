Biography

I spent nearly 17 years in fundraising. While I occasionally worked directly with donors, most of my roles were “behind the scenes”. My favorite workdays were always the ones I spent:

  • Solving problems
  • Understanding and organizing details
  • Developing or following processes
  • Writing
  • Presenting
  • Learning new software
I often wished I had a more technical skill set to offer, which helped me decide to change careers from fund development to web development in 2021.

I’m currently enrolled in a three-part web development bootcamp through MIT xPRO. I learned HTML, CSS and JavaScript in the first course, and look forward to tackling the front-end development with React next, followed by back-end development. I’ll finish my studies in April 2022.
